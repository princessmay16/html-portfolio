Sinigang na Baboy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, peeled and sliced
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 bundle string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 radish (labanos), peeled and sliced
  • 1 bunch water spinach (kangkong), separated into stalks and leaves
  • 2 liters water
  • 1 packet (40g) sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil pork: In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add pork belly and simmer until meat is tender. Skim off any impurities that rise to the surface.
  2. Add vegetables: Once the pork is tender, add sliced onions, tomatoes, and radish. Simmer until vegetables are slightly softened.
  3. Sinigang mix: Add the sinigang mix to the pot. Adjust the quantity based on your preferred level of sourness. Stir well.
  4. Seasoning: Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper according to your taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
  5. Vegetable addition: Add the string beans and eggplants to the pot. Simmer until the vegetables are cooked but still crisp.
  6. Leafy vegetables: Add the water spinach (kangkong) last, including both the stalks and leaves. Simmer until the kangkong is just wilted.
  7. Serve: Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve hot with steamed rice.
